## Do's
1.Regularly check who can see your posts. Keep personal life private and professional achievements public...

2.Post updates about your coding projects, hackathon participation, or progress on Project Euler challenges to build a "digital portfolio."

3.Follow and engage with engineering professionals and companies you admire on LinkedIn.
 
4.Always use Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) to protect your accounts from being compromised.

5.Fact-check technical resources or campus news before hitting "repost" to maintain your credibility among batchmates.

## Don't
1.Never post rants about professors, difficult assignments, or campus grievances; these can be seen by future recruiters.

2.Don't share your phone number, hostel address, or photos of your ID cards/hall tickets.

3.Avoid using aliases like shadow_coder or party_king on platforms you link to your resume or GitHub.

4.Don't upload photos or videos of your friends or lab partners without asking for their permission first.

5.Don't post anything you wouldn't want a hiring manager to find during a background check.
